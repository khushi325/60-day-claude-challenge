🚀 Day 23 – Customer & MVP Blueprint with Claude AI
📌 Overview

On Day 23 of the 60 Days Claude AI Challenge, I focused on transforming my validated startup idea into a customer-centric business plan.

Building upon the Startup Validation Report from Day 22, I used Claude AI to analyze my target audience, identify customer pain points, design an Ideal Customer Profile (ICP), map the customer journey, and define a practical Minimum Viable Product (MVP).

The generated report also included pricing assumptions, feature prioritization using the MoSCoW Framework, risk analysis, and a founder action plan for validating the product with real users.

🎯 Objective
Define the Ideal Customer Profile (ICP).
Understand customer needs and buying behavior.
Identify the most critical pain points.
Design a practical MVP.
Prioritize features using the MoSCoW framework.
Build a customer-first product roadmap.

💡 Key Learnings
Successful startups begin with understanding customers rather than building features.
Customer pain points should drive product development.
MVPs should focus on solving one core problem exceptionally well.
Feature prioritization prevents unnecessary complexity.
Early customer validation reduces product and market risks.
🌟 Biggest Insight

Great startups don't start with perfect products—they start with a deep understanding of customer problems and continuously improve through feedback.
