# 🚨 Day 31 – AI Supply Chain Control Tower with Claude AI

## 📌 Overview

On **Day 31** of the **60 Days Claude AI Challenge**, I built and explored an interactive **AI Supply Chain Control Tower** using Claude AI.

The simulator replicates a real-time supply chain monitoring center where users act as operations managers responsible for handling disruptions across suppliers, logistics, inventory, warehouses, and customer deliveries. Throughout the simulation, incoming operational alerts must be prioritized and resolved while monitoring their impact on key business KPIs.

This challenge provided practical experience in supply chain visibility, operational decision-making, and AI-assisted business management.

---

# 🎯 Objective

- Understand the concept of a Supply Chain Control Tower.
- Monitor real-time operational alerts.
- Prioritize incidents based on urgency.
- Select the most effective corrective actions.
- Observe KPI changes after every decision.
- Improve operational efficiency through AI-assisted monitoring.

---

# 🚨 About the Simulator

The AI Supply Chain Control Tower is an interactive operational dashboard that simulates real-world supply chain management.

The simulator includes:

- Real-time operational alerts
- Incident prioritization
- Corrective action selection
- Live KPI updates
- Multiple simultaneous disruptions
- Operational performance dashboard
- Replay functionality for different scenarios

---

# 🔄 Simulation Workflow

The simulation follows these stages:

1. Read Instructions
2. Monitor Incoming Alerts
3. Prioritize Critical Incidents
4. Choose Corrective Actions
5. Observe KPI Changes
6. Handle Multiple Operational Issues
7. Complete Simulation
8. Review Operational Performance Dashboard
9. Replay with New Scenarios

---

# 🚚 Operational Alerts

The simulator generates various operational challenges, including:

- Supplier delays
- Inventory shortages
- Warehouse bottlenecks
- Transportation disruptions
- Delivery delays
- Production slowdowns
- Demand spikes
- Logistics failures

Each alert requires a timely and strategic response.

---

# ⚙️ Corrective Actions

Possible response actions include:

- Expedite shipments
- Activate backup suppliers
- Reallocate inventory
- Increase production capacity
- Optimize transportation routes
- Prioritize customer orders
- Adjust warehouse operations
- Improve demand forecasting

Each decision directly impacts business performance.

---

# 📊 KPI Dashboard

The simulator continuously tracks key performance indicators such as:

- On-Time Delivery

- Customer Satisfaction
- Operational Efficiency
- Supply Chain Risk

- Cost Efficiency

# 📈 Final Operational Dashboard

At the end of the simulation, the dashboard summarizes:
