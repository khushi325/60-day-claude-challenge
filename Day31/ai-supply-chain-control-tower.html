How to Play

Your Role

You're the Head of Operations. Alerts appear on the left describing real supply-chain incidents. Each one has a priority, a countdown, and a business impact.

Taking Action

  • Every alert offers several response actions plus Ignore and Delay Decision.
  • The right action improves KPIs and increases your score. The wrong action for that specific incident costs you KPIs and cost efficiency.
  • Some outcomes are delayed — you'll see a toast notification a few seconds later showing the consequence.
  • If an alert's timer runs out before you act, it resolves itself badly.

KPIs

Service Level, Customer Satisfaction, Inventory Health and Transportation Efficiency should stay high. Operating Cost should stay low. Revenue Protected accumulates from good decisions.

Difficulty

Alerts arrive faster and stack up more as the 3-minute shift progresses. Stay sharp near the end.
