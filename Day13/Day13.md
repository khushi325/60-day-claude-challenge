# 🚀 Day 13 – AI-Powered Job Discovery with Claude & Indeed Connector

## 📌 Overview

On **Day 13** of the **60 Days Claude AI Challenge**, I explored how AI can simplify the job search process by using **Claude Connectors** and the **Indeed Connector** to discover relevant internship and entry-level opportunities.
