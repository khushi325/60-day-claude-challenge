# 🚀 Day 8 – Building an Environmental Health Analyzer Dashboard

## 📌 Overview

On **Day 8** of the **60 Days Claude AI Challenge**, I explored how AI can assist in building complete, interactive applications from a single prompt.

For this challenge, I used the **Environmental Health Analyzer Prompt** to generate a fully functional **HTML dashboard** that analyzes environmental health indicators such as air quality, water quality, health scores, and city-wise comparisons.

This exercise demonstrated how AI can go beyond generating text and help create real-world applications, dashboards, and data-driven experiences.
