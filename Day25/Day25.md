# 🦈 Day 25 – AI Shark Tank Simulator with Claude AI

## 📌 Overview

On **Day 25** of the **60 Days Claude AI Challenge**, I experienced the role of a startup founder by pitching my idea to an AI-powered Shark Tank simulation.

Using Claude AI, I generated an interactive HTML application that simulated a real investor pitch session. The simulator evaluated my startup idea through multiple AI investors, asked challenging business questions, calculated an investment scorecard, estimated valuation, and provided funding recommendations.

This activity helped me understand how investors evaluate startups based on problem-solving ability, market opportunity, business model, scalability, and founder vision.

---

# 🎯 Objective

- Simulate a real startup investor pitch.
- Generate an interactive AI Shark Tank application.
- Evaluate startup readiness.
- Practice answering investor questions.
- Understand valuation and funding recommendations.
- Learn how investors assess startup potential.

# 🌱 Startup Presented

## Startup Name

**Bhumiputra**

### Tagline

**AI-Powered Smart Soil Analysis & Crop Recommendation Platform**

### Elevator Pitch

Bhumiputra is an AI-powered platform that enables farmers to identify soil types using image classification and receive personalized crop recommendations, fertilizer suggestions, and sustainable farming guidance. The platform aims to make precision agriculture affordable, accessible, and easy to use for farmers.

# 🦈 AI Shark Tank Simulation

The AI Shark Tank simulator evaluated the startup from multiple investor perspectives.

### Judges Evaluated

- Product Expert
- Market Expert
- Financial Expert
- Technology Expert
- Growth Investor

Each judge challenged different aspects of the business, encouraging deeper thinking about the startup strategy.

---

# ❓ Sample Investor Questions

### Product Judge

- What unique value does your product provide?
- Why would customers choose your solution over competitors?

### Market Judge

- How large is your target market?
- What is your customer acquisition strategy?

### Financial Judge

- What is your expected revenue model?
- How will the startup become profitable?

### Technology Judge

- Is your AI model scalable?
- How will you improve prediction accuracy over time?

### Growth Investor

- What is your long-term vision?
- How will you expand internationally?
