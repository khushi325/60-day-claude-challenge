# 🚀 Day 3 Overview – Role-Based Prompting

On **Day 3** of the **60-Day Claude AI Challenge**, I explored the concept of **Role-Based Prompting**—one of the most effective prompt engineering techniques for improving AI responses.

🎯 **Objective:** Understand how assigning different roles to AI changes the quality, perspective, and depth of its responses.

💬 I asked the **same career-related question** in three different ways:

* 🤖 Without assigning any role
* 🚀 As an **AI Startup Founder**
* 💻 As a **Senior AI Engineer**

🔍 By comparing all three responses, I observed how each persona brought a unique perspective—ranging from general guidance to business strategy and technical expertise.

📊 I also explored the **Claude Usage Counter** extension, documented my prompts, captured screenshots, recorded key observations, and summarized my learnings in this repository.

💡 **Key Takeaway:** *The role you assign to AI directly influences the quality, relevance, and expertise of the response. Better context leads to better conversations with AI.*
