🚀 Day 16 – Building a Custom Stock Fundamental Research Skill with Claude AI
📌 Overview

On Day 16 of the 60 Days Claude AI Challenge, I explored one of Claude AI's most powerful features—Custom Skills.

Instead of repeatedly writing long prompts, I created a reusable AI skill named stock-fundamental-research to perform structured fundamental analysis of Indian and global listed companies.

After creating the skill, I tested it by analyzing and comparing stocks, allowing Claude to generate comprehensive reports covering valuation, financial performance, ownership trends, business quality, peer comparison, and risk analysis.

This challenge demonstrated how reusable AI workflows can significantly improve productivity and consistency for research-intensive tasks.

🎯 Objective
Learn how to create reusable Custom Skills in Claude.
Build a Stock Fundamental Research Assistant.
Analyze companies using financial fundamentals.
Generate structured and evidence-based research reports.
Understand AI-powered workflow automation.
