# 📈 Day 32 – Marketing Strategy Simulator with Claude AI

## 📌 Overview

On **Day 32** of the **60 Days Claude AI Challenge**, I built and explored an interactive **Marketing Strategy Simulator** using Claude AI.

This simulator helps users create a complete marketing strategy by understanding the target audience, selecting the most effective marketing platforms, defining content pillars, and generating a structured 30-day marketing roadmap. It also introduces unexpected marketing events, allowing users to adapt their strategy and improve business growth.

The challenge demonstrated how AI can assist marketers, entrepreneurs, and personal brands in making data-driven marketing decisions through an engaging interactive experience.

---

# 🎯 Objective

- Understand the fundamentals of marketing strategy.
- Identify the ideal target audience.
- Select the most effective marketing channels.
- Build strong content pillars.
- Generate a structured 30-day marketing roadmap.
- Respond to unexpected marketing situations.
- Analyze business growth through AI-generated insights.

---

# 📊 About the Simulator

The Marketing Strategy Simulator guides users through the complete marketing planning process.

The simulator includes:

- Business, Personal Brand, or Random Client mode
- Target audience analysis
- Marketing platform selection
- Content pillar planning
- AI-generated 30-day marketing roadmap
- Random marketing events
- Growth performance report
- Reusable Claude prompts for future marketing planning

---

# 🔄 Simulation Workflow

The simulation follows these stages:

1. Choose Business Mode
2. Understand the Target Audience
3. Select Marketing Platforms
4. Choose Three Content Pillars
5. Generate a 30-Day Marketing Roadmap
6. Respond to a Random Marketing Event
7. Review the Final Growth Report
8. Explore Reusable Claude Prompts
9. Replay with Different Businesses or Personal Brands
