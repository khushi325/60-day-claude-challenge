# 🚀 Day 11 – ATS Resume Optimization with Claude AI

## 📌 Overview

On **Day 11** of the **60 Days Claude AI Challenge**, I explored how AI can be used to analyze and optimize resumes for **Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS)**.

I uploaded my resume and compared it against a target **AI/ML Engineer Intern Job Description** to understand how recruiters and ATS systems evaluate resumes. Claude generated an ATS analysis, identified skill gaps, and suggested improvements to make my resume more competitive.

This challenge highlighted the importance of tailoring a resume to specific job roles and using the right keywords to improve visibility during the hiring process.

# 📊 ATS Analysis Summary

### Target Role

**AI/ML Engineer Intern**

### Estimated ATS Match Score

**84/100**

### Strengths

* Strong foundation in Java, Python, SQL, and Machine Learning.
* Relevant projects in AI, Data Visualization, and Application Development.
* Government AI/ML Internship experience.
* Industry certifications and active GitHub profile.

### Identified Gaps

* Missing keywords such as:

  * Scikit-learn
  * NumPy
  * Deep Learning
  * REST APIs
  * Generative AI
  * LLM Applications
  * Model Evaluation
  * Feature Engineering

* Projects could be strengthened by adding measurable achievements and impact metrics.

# 💡 Key Learnings

* A resume should be customized for every job role.
* ATS systems prioritize relevant keywords and skills.
* Quantifiable project achievements improve resume effectiveness.
* Personal branding and project presentation play a major role in getting shortlisted.
* AI can significantly simplify resume optimization.

# 🌟 Biggest Insight
