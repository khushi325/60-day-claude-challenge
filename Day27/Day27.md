# 🏥 Day 27 – Prior Authorization Story Simulator with Claude AI

## 📌 Overview

On **Day 27** of the **60 Days Claude AI Challenge**, I built and explored an interactive **Prior Authorization Story Simulator** using Claude AI.

Unlike the previous workflow simulator, this project presents the healthcare authorization process as a story. The learner follows Rahul's journey while Priya explains every step, making complex healthcare operations easier to understand through storytelling.

The simulator combines interactive choices, educational dialogue, progress tracking, and branching story paths to create an engaging learning experience.

---

# 🎯 Objective

- Learn the Prior Authorization process through storytelling.
- Understand the patient's healthcare journey.
- Explore decision-making at each stage.
- Experience an interactive educational simulation.
- Learn healthcare workflows in an engaging way.

---

# 📖 Story Overview

The simulator follows **Rahul**, a patient requiring medical treatment.

Throughout the story:

- Rahul encounters different healthcare situations.
- Priya explains every stage of the authorization process.
- The learner chooses how the story progresses.
- The progress bar updates after every chapter.
- The journey ends with treatment approval and key healthcare lessons.

---

# 🛤 Story Journey

The interactive story contains **8 chapters**:

1. Healthcare Need
2. Doctor Consultation
3. Medical Evaluation
4. Prior Authorization Requirement
5. Documentation Submission
6. Insurance Review
7. Authorization Decision
8. Treatment Approval & Final Takeaways

---

# 🎮 Interactive Features

- Story-based healthcare learning
- Rahul and Priya dialogue
- Two decision choices after every chapter
- Dynamic story progression
- Progress tracker
- Educational explanations
- Multiple dialogue paths
- Restart option

---

# 📊 Learning Outcomes

Through this simulator I learned:

- Why Prior Authorization exists.
- How providers communicate with insurance companies.
- The importance of complete documentation.
- How patients experience the authorization process.
- How AI can make healthcare education more interactive.

---

# 💡 Biggest Learning

Storytelling makes complex healthcare workflows much easier to understand than traditional documentation.

---

# 🌟 Biggest Insight

> AI can transform technical healthcare concepts into engaging interactive stories that improve learning and user engagement.

---

# ✅ Conclusion

Day 27 demonstrated how AI can combine storytelling and interactive learning to simplify complex healthcare workflows. Instead of reading documentation, learners actively participate in the patient's journey, making the concepts easier to understand and remember.

### 🚀 Key Takeaway

AI-powered storytelling can significantly improve healthcare education by turning complex administrative processes into engaging learning experiences.
