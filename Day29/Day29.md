# 🚚 Day 29 – Operation Lifeline: AI Supply Chain Crisis Simulator

## 📌 Overview

On **Day 29** of the **60 Days Claude AI Challenge**, I built and explored **Operation Lifeline**, an AI-powered Supply Chain Crisis Simulator developed using React and HTML.

The simulator places the user in the role of a supply chain leader responsible for managing unexpected business disruptions. Every simulation generates a unique company profile and crisis scenario, requiring strategic decision-making to minimize business impact and maintain operational continuity.

Through supplier negotiations, leadership decisions, AI investment planning, and executive dashboard analysis, I gained practical insights into modern supply chain management and business resilience.

---

# 🎯 Objective

- Understand real-world supply chain disruptions.
- Practice crisis management decision-making.
- Improve supplier negotiation skills.
- Learn executive leadership during emergencies.
- Evaluate AI-powered supply chain investments.
- Analyze business performance using executive dashboards.

---

# 🚚 About Operation Lifeline

Operation Lifeline is an interactive business simulation where players manage supply chain crises affecting manufacturing and logistics operations.

The simulator includes:

- Random company profiles
- Dynamic crisis scenarios
- Supplier negotiation rounds
- CEO leadership decisions
- AI investment planning
- Executive performance dashboard
- Business score analysis
- Replayable simulations

---

# 🔄 Simulation Workflow

The simulation follows these stages:

1. Generate Company Profile
2. Analyze Supply Chain Crisis
3. Select Three Crisis Response Actions
4. Complete Supplier Negotiation Rounds
5. Answer CEO Leadership Questions
6. Choose Two AI Supply Chain Investments
7. Review Executive Dashboard
8. Analyze Business Performance
9. Restart with New Crisis Scenario

---

# ⚠ Crisis Scenarios

Possible challenges include:

- Supplier bankruptcy

Each scenario requires a unique strategic response.
