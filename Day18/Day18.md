🚀 Day 18 – Brain Dump Action Planner with Claude AI
📌 Overview

On Day 18 of the 60 Days Claude AI Challenge, I explored Claude AI's Custom Skills by creating a reusable workflow called brain-dump-action-planner.

The purpose of this skill is to transform unstructured information—such as meeting notes, brainstorming sessions, voice transcripts, class notes, and project discussions—into organized summaries, actionable tasks, timelines, risks, blockers, and interactive dashboards.

This challenge demonstrated how AI can significantly improve productivity by organizing scattered ideas into clear execution plans.
