# 🚀 Day 24 – Business Strategy Blueprint with Claude AI

## 📌 Overview

On **Day 24** of the **60 Days Claude AI Challenge**, I transformed my validated startup idea into a complete business strategy.

Building on the **Customer & MVP Blueprint** from Day 23, I used Claude AI to develop a comprehensive business strategy for **Bhumiputra – AI-Powered Smart Soil Analysis & Crop Recommendation System**. The report covered the Business Model Canvas, revenue strategy, customer acquisition plan, competitive positioning, investment readiness, and a roadmap for acquiring the first 100 users.

This challenge shifted my thinking from **building a product** to **building a sustainable business**.
